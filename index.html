ABOUT ME

This is a practice website, designed with a soul purpose of bettering my front-end skills. I developed a passion for technology and computers at a young age, but did not do any actual development until I started Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. The development that I was introduced to was mostly back-end, and began with C and Java. Throughout my four years at the university I realized how wide the world of developement really was, and it was not until I had my first post-college job that I got interested in front-end development; mostly because the company I work for wanted our team to be better rouded so they placed us in a webdevelopment bootcamp (hosted by UCF). After a few weeks in the bootcamp, I realized that I enjoyed front-end development more than back-end. Outside of my professional life, I'm originally from Sweden, born and raised, but moved to Florida at the age of 20 to pursue a professional golf career. I played a lot of golf in high school. The team I played on won nationals and got to compete in the St. Andrews University Open with various other eurpean teams. After the tough realization that golf was extemely challenging on a professional level, I decided to keep it as a hobby, and switched focus to my degree. I have now lived in Orlando, Florida, for 7 years with my two cats.
